About Me

I work at the intersection of data modeling, analysis, and visualization, with hands-on experience across both public and private sector projects. Using tools like SQL and Power BI, I've delivered impactful insights that support clear and data-driven decisions.

I've also worked extensively with 3D city data, GML formats, and data transformation using FME—building a strong technical foundation in structured data workflows and spatial thinking.

My current focus is expanding my expertise in data engineering and AI-supported systems. I'm confident in SQL and database processes, and I actively use Python for data analysis and interpretation.

My goal is to create efficient, meaningful solutions by combining real-world data experience with emerging technologies and to keep growing in the evolving world of data.
